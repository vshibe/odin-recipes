Lasagna recipe

Description

A recipe for lasagna, lifted from smittenkitchen.com.

Ingredients

Bolognese sauce

  • 1 medium onion, coarsely chopped (1-inch pieces are fine)
  • 1 large or 2 slim carrots, coarsely chopped
  • 2 ribs celery, coarsely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds ground beef or, if you prefer, 1 pound each beef and pork
  • 1 1/4 cups tomato paste (from 2 6-ounce cans)
  • 2 cups red wine, preferably hearty but really, anything you like to drink
  • Water as needed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • A few sprigs thyme, tied in a bundle

Pasta

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea or table salt
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons water, if needed

Béchamel sauce

  • 1/2 cup (8 tablespoons) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea or table salt
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • Freshly grated nutmeg, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Assembly

  • 1 2/3 cups grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

Day 1: Bolognese Sauce

  1. Chop vegetables: Pulse onion, carrots, celery, and garlic in a food processor.
  2. Cook veggies: Sauté in a Dutch oven with oil until browned, ~15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Brown beef: Add ground beef, season, and cook until browned, ~15 minutes.
  4. Add flavors: Mix in tomato paste, cook for 3-4 minutes. Add red wine and reduce, ~5 minutes.
  5. Simmer sauce: Add water (1 inch above meat), bay leaves, and thyme. Simmer for 3-4 hours, adding water as needed. Makes about 8 cups; refrigerate 4 cups for lasagna.

Day 2: Pasta and Béchamel

  1. Make pasta dough: Combine ingredients in a food processor, rest for 1 hour.
  2. Roll and cook pasta: Roll dough to thin sheets, boil cut pieces for 1-2 minutes, then cool in ice water.
  3. Make béchamel: Melt butter, add flour, cook 1 minute. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth, cook for 10 minutes. Season.

Assemble Lasagna

  1. Preheat oven: 400°F (200°C).
  2. Layer ingredients: In a 9*13-inch dish, layer pasta, bolognese, béchamel, and parmesan. Repeat 4 times.
  3. Top and bake: Finish with béchamel and parmesan, bake 30-45 minutes until bubbly and browned.
  4. Rest and serve: Let lasagna rest for 10-30 minutes before serving.

Do-ahead tips: Assemble lasagna a day in advance or freeze before baking. Reheats well for up to 3 days.
